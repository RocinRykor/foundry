## Claude as Seed Data
Now I get to the heart of what I think LLMs are how we should be using them
A LLM, in my mind, cannot create anything it took tons and tons training data and spits out the most generic slop it can, that is not creative that is not art.

So instead, you take what Claude gives you learn from it, which I know is hilarious given that we benchmark AIs by how badly they hallucinate all the times, but there in lies the answer.. what did it do and why, more importantly, how did it fuck up, because its not human it cant create anything it can only recreate and in the recreation it has flaws. You keep learning what mistakes it made, eventually you get to a point where you realize it would be easier to do the thing yourself rather than try to struggle with the AI to circle it around a problem its never going to fix.

If you are using Claude, you need to be the biggest critic of your own work. and use it to [[Learn]] not just recreate slop for no use.
